My name is Emma Weber and I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I'm currently in a 12 week Full Stack, full-time coding boot camp at the University of Denver. After a few years working for a start up in downtown Denver I've decided to change career paths and challenge myself to try something new! My interest in coding was encouraged by a project I worked on with the Eng team in my most recent position--the colors, the layering, the ability to build something with creativity and skill, all caught my attention.

I've always had a knack for problem solving whether it was finding the answer to a riddle, figuring out who the killer was in 'Criminal Minds' before the episode was over, or how to pack a U-haul perfectly with all of my belongings. There is also a creative spark in me that I can't ignore. I dabble with typography, wish I could be Joanna Gaines, and make homemade Christmas cards for friends and family. These two interests have melded together in how I code. I thrive when solving problems on the back end and can spend hours perfecting my CSS.
